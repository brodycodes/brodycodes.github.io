Why UI / UX Design?

I've always been very interested in how people interact with the things in our world. My first influencial experience with this was seeing how myself and others interacted with websites. At the time I became aware of my deep interest in UX, I was deciding whether I wanted to attend college to pursue graphic design or illustration. I chose the former with the thought that I could use my visual creativity to influence user experience. It seemed logical at that point to study user interface design, which was easy to find interest in. My current goal is to better understand various programming languages while developing my visual art skills.

I work hard to provide meaningful, well-designed content that is not only visually appealing, but also engaging in a way that's both easy to use and effectively solves a problem. I strive for quality, integrity, and a tailored approach so that I can better use my creative problem-solving skills to efficiently achieve the desired results.

Outside of my professional interests, I enjoy cycling, trading card games, and board games. I'm an auto enthusiast, mostly fond of working on Volkswagens and Audis. I also have an interest in pc building.
