Why UI / UX Design?

I've always been very interested in how people interact with the things in our world. My first real moving experience with this was seeing how myself and others interacted with websites. At the time I became aware of my deep interest in UX I was deciding whether I wanted to attend college and pursue graphic design, or illustration. I chose the former with the thought that I could use my visual creativity to influence user-experience

I strive to create profound and meaningful solutions that put the human element at the core, where effective and applicable changes can emerge. I've been fortunate to be able to connect unrelated dots in my past for this purpose. I now thoroughly enjoy being at the crossroads of business, technology, and design in the context of healthcare.

Outside of the digital world, you can find me throwing clay in a pottery class, hiking with my dog, or globe trotting.
